Purpose and Scope

CCQ is workshop on different topics of theory of computation, computational compexity and cryptography. It will be exactly before CSR 2017 in Kazan, Russia.

The topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Quantum and classic computing
  • Cryptogrphy
  • Complexity classes
  • Circuit complexity
  • Algebraic complexity
  • Proof complexity
  • Interactive and probabilistic proof systems
  • Complexity in other concrete computational models
  • Logic and descriptive complexity
  • Average case complexity
  • Communication complexity
